(1) In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires—

"Chairperson"
means the Chairperson of the Authority;
"Chief Executive"
means the Chief Executive of the Authority appointed under section 8;
"member"
means a member of the Authority;
"Minister"
means the Minister charged with the responsibility for this Act.
