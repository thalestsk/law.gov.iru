Crown Seneschalty Order 2020

An Order of State for the establishment of the Crown Seneschalty as the institution for the Sovereign's personal affairs.
Reference No: OS.2020/01 Enacted: 1 January 2020

By Order of His Imperial Majesty Emperor Thales XIII, Sovereign of the State of Common Good

1. Short title, etc.

This Order of State is the Crown Seneschalty Order 2020 as promulgated on 1 January 2020.

2. Crown Seneschalty

The Crown Seneschalty is hereby established as the institution for the Sovereign's personal affairs. The institution of the Crown Seneschalty encompasses the entirety of the personal life, affairs and properties of the Sovereign and the Royal Family.

3. Pleasure of the Sovereign

All positions and offices within the Crown Seneschalty are held at the pleasure of the Sovereign. The Sovereign has sole discretion to appoint, suspend, or remove any office or officeholder from the Crown Seneschalty.

4. Grand Seneschal

The Grand Seneschal shall be the chief executive officer of the Crown Seneschalty.

5. Crown Constable

(1) The Crown Constable shall be the commander of the Royal Household Guards.

(2) The Crown Constable is administratively subordinate to the Grand Seneschal and operationally accountable to the Sovereign.

(3) The Crown Constable shall organise, maintain and develop the Royal Household Guards to ensure the security and protection of the Sovereign and the Crown Seneschalty.

6. Crown Steward

(1) The Crown Steward shall be the head of the Royal Household Staff.

(2) The Crown Steward is administratively subordinate to the Grand Seneschal and operationally accountable to the Sovereign and the Royal Family.

(3) The Crown Steward shall organise, maintain and develop the Royal Household Staff to ensure the efficient and effective management of the private offices and personal wellbeing of the Sovereign and the Royal Family.

7. Crown Chancellor

(1) The Crown Chancellor shall be the head of the Privy Chanchery.

(2) The Crown Chancellor is administratively subordinate to the Grand Seneschal and operationally accountable to the Sovereign.

(3) The Crown Chancellor shall organise, maintain and develop the Privy Chanchery to enable the Sovereign to exercise its absolute authority over the Domain and its affairs.

8. Crown Proprietor

(1) The Crown Proprietor shall serve as the chief executive officer of the Crown Propriety.

(2) The Crown Proprietor shall report directly to the Grand Seneschal.

(3) The Crown Proprietor shall organise, maintain and develop the Crown Propriety to safeguard and further the corporate and commercial interests of the assets and properties pertaining to the institutional standing of the Sovereign and the Royal Family.

(4) The Crown Proprietor shall advise the Sovereign on any request by the Government pertaining to the assets and properties under the Crown Propriety, subject to the sole discretion of the Sovereign.

(5) The Crown Proprietor shall, with the concurrence of the Grand Seneschal, disburse monies from the Crown Propriety to the Crown Seneschalty for the effective operation of the Crown Seneschalty.

(6) The Crown Proprietor shall ensure that the Crown Propriety has the ability to generate sufficient revenue for the disbursement of monies to the Crown Seneschalty without impeding the long-term sustainability of the Crown Propriety.

9. Crown Treasurer

(1) The Crown Treasurer shall serve as the chief executive officer of the Sovereign Fount.

(2) The Crown Treasurer shall report directly to the Grand Seneschal.

(3) The Crown Treasurer shall organise, maintain and develop the Sovereign Fount to safeguard and further the personal financial interests of the Sovereign and the Royal Family.

(4) The Crown Treasurer shall, at the sole discretion of the Sovereign, make a contribution from the Sovereign Fount to the Government to support the state of public finances.

(5) The Crown Treasurer shall ensure that the Sovereign Fount is managed in a manner that is exemplary of the standards of corporate governance that the Sovereign expects of the entrepeneurial and business spheres of the Domain.

10. Privy Council

Where the Sovereign has constituted a Privy Council through which he exercises his absolute authority over the Domain, the Crown Chancellor shall make the necessary arrangements in support of the Privy Council such that the Privy Council is an extension of the Privy Chancery, but hierarchically of equal standing to the Grand Seneschal.

11. Ceremonial Precedence

The Royal Family by virtue of their blood relation to the Sovereign shall take ceremonial precedence over the Crown Seneschalty and its officers. Ceremonial precedence within the Crown Seneschalty and the Government thereafter shall be determined by the Crown Steward, with the concurrence of the Grand Seneschal.

12. Sovereign Authority

Apart from the Sovereign and the persons authorised in this Order of State, no other person or office may provide for matters relating to and under the purview of the Crown Seneschalty.
