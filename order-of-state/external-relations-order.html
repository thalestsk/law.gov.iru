External Relations Order 2026

An Order of State for the establishment of the Directorate-General of External Relations.
Reference No: OS.2026/01 Enacted: 1 January 2026

By Order of His Imperial Majesty Emperor Thales XIII, Sovereign of the State of Common Good

1. Short title, etc.

This Order of State is the External Relations Order 2026 as promulgated on 1 January 2026.

2. Directorate-General of External Relations

The Directorate-General of External Relations (the "Directorate-General") is hereby established as the umbrella organisation for all executive functions pertaining to external relations and foreign affairs.

3. Director-General

(1) The Director-General of External Relations ("Director-General") is the chief executive officer of the Directorate-General and shall be appointed by the Sovereign.

(2) The Director-General shall be the highest functionary representing the Sovereign in matters of external relations, and shall act as the principal authority through whom the Sovereign's will is expressed and administered in such matters.

(3) The Director-General may appoint other persons and organise accordingly for the effective administration of the Directorate-General.

(4) The Director-General shall appoint high representatives to foreign states and international organisations with the concurrence of the Sovereign.

4. General Functions

The Director-General shall, as the principal intermediary between the Sovereign and third parties beyond the State, exercise the following functions:

(a) represent and communicate the position, interests, and intentions of the State externally;

(b) receive, interpret, and convey communications, positions, and developments from external persons, entities, and authorities to the State;

(c) facilitate, manage, and maintain relations between the State and foreign states, international organisations, and other external actors;

(d) protect and advance the interests of the State and its citizens in external matters;

(e) ensure consistency, coherence, and continuity in the external conduct of the State; and

(f) perform such acts and functions as are necessary or incidental to the discharge of the Director-General's role as intermediary in external relations.

5. Estates-General

The Estates-General shall provide for the procedures, protocols and standards insofar as they relate to the conduct of external relations and foreign affairs, such that they are consistent with the principles of the Order of State, without encumbering the effectiveness of the Director-General's general functions.

6. Direct Responsibility to Sovereign

The Director-General bears sole, direct and personal responsibility to the Sovereign until otherwise discharged by the Sovereign.
